About Insulo.app

Free · Offline · No registration · Open since 2026

What is Insulo.app?

Insulo.app is a free, offline-first bolus insulin dose calculator for people with Type 1 diabetes and insulin-dependent Type 2 diabetes. It calculates how much rapid-acting insulin to take before a meal, accounting for both carbohydrate intake and blood glucose correction.

The app uses the standard bolus formula: Total dose = Meal dose (Carbs ÷ ICR) + Correction dose ((Current BG − Target BG) ÷ ISF). Results are rounded to the nearest 0.5 unit for practical use.

How is Insulo.app different from other insulin calculators?

Looking for the best free insulin calculator? Insulo.app is a free, no-account-required alternative to mySugr, BolusCalc, and other bolus calculators — with the key advantage of working fully offline. Once loaded, it works without any internet connection. All data stays locally in the browser. There is no registration, no email, no password.

Unlike apps embedded in CGM ecosystems (Dexcom, Libre, mySugr), Insulo.app is device-agnostic — it works with any glucose meter or CGM, or with manually entered values.

Who is Insulo.app for?

Insulo.app is designed for people on multiple daily injections (MDI) — basal + bolus insulin therapy. This includes most people with Type 1 diabetes and insulin-dependent Type 2 diabetes. The calculator requires ICR and ISF values which are set by your endocrinologist.

The app is an informational tool only and is not a certified medical device. All dosing decisions are made by the patient and their healthcare provider.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free offline insulin calculator?

Insulo.app is one of the only bolus insulin calculators that works completely offline, requires no account or registration, and is free with no subscription. It is available at insulo.app and can be installed on iPhone and Android as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Is there a free alternative to mySugr bolus calculator?

Yes. Insulo.app is a free alternative to the mySugr Bolus Calculator (which requires a PRO subscription). Insulo.app uses the same standard bolus formula (ICR + ISF), works offline, and requires no account. Unlike mySugr, all data stays locally on the device.

Does Insulo.app work without internet?

Yes. After the first visit, Insulo.app is cached by the browser's service worker and works completely without an internet connection. This makes it reliable in areas with poor connectivity, on flights, or in hospitals with restricted Wi-Fi.

Can I use this insulin calculator without creating an account?

Yes. Insulo.app requires no registration, no email address, and no password. Open the app and start calculating immediately. No account required.
